Creature Simulation

This site allows you observe a simulation of creatures in an environment where you can control various aspects of both the environment and creature behaviors

Things to Note:

Food is represented by green squares. It does not move

Hives are represented by square outlines. They will create creatures of their outlines color

Creatures are represented by squares and/or triangles and will eat food which increases their size

Creatures of different colors will attempt to consume each other if they can find a smaller creature and there is no more food

When creature has eaten enough food to raise it's size to 60 it will split into 4 creatures each with a size of 15

How to Use:

Select what you would like to spawn in the Spawn Type section

This will allow you to customize aspects of the entity before you spawn it

Simply click on canvas to create the entity at your mouse point
